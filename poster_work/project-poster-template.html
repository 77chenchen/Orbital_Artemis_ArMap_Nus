Atlas AR Campus Assistant

A context-aware campus navigation assistant that helps NUS students move from outdoor routes to indoor facilities, using AR-inspired wayfinding, schedule-aware recommendations, and live campus data.

ProblemNUS navigation often stops at the building entrance.
GoalGuide students to rooms, lifts, study spaces, restrooms, and next activities.
UsersStudents moving between classes, study areas, and daily campus routines.
OutputWeb demo + Go API + SQLite-backed campus data.

Overview

Atlas targets the gap between generic maps and real student movement. The app combines geospatial campus points, indoor facility discovery, personal schedule entries, and recommendation logic so students receive useful guidance based on location, time, and campus context.

Campus DataBuildings, floors, facilities, crowd hints
ScheduleClasses, meetings, locations, time windows
ContextCurrent location and next destination
GuidanceRoute and facility recommendations

Why It Matters

  • Students need help after reaching the correct building, not only before.
  • Indoor amenities such as lifts, restrooms, printers, and study spaces affect daily routines.
  • Schedule-aware recommendations reduce planning overhead during busy school days.
